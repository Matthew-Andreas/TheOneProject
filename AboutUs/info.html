The ONE Project is a centralized resource hub designed to help entrepreneurs in finding the resources they need to start, develop, or exit their businesses. ONE Hub SD focuses on resources for entrepreneurs in the San Diego region.

We are a multi-disciplinary team of California State University San Marcos professors and students working to help entrepreneurs and small business owners succeed. We recognize that different entrepreneurs have varying needs based on their backgrounds and ventures, so we have developed a guide that helps them find precisely the resources they need. With this project, we hope to make the entrepreneurial ecosystem more accessible to all entrepreneurs.
